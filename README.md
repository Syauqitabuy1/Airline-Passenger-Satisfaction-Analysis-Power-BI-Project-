# ✈️ Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis (Power BI Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes airline passenger satisfaction using a real-world dataset from Kaggle:
[https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/teejmahal20/airline-passenger-satisfaction](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/teejmahal20/airline-passenger-satisfaction)

As a **Data Analyst / Product Analyst**, I approached this dataset as if I were working for an airline company facing declining customer satisfaction and retention issues.

The goal is to identify key drivers of passenger satisfaction and provide actionable business insights.

---

## 🧩 Business Case

### 🏢 Background

An airline company has recently experienced:

* Decreasing customer satisfaction scores
* Increasing customer complaints
* Declining repeat purchases from passengers

Management suspects that service quality across different touchpoints (onboard, airport, and digital experience) is inconsistent.

---

### ❗ Problem Statement

> What factors most influence passenger satisfaction, and how can the airline improve its service to increase customer retention?

---

### 🎯 Objectives

* Identify key variables affecting passenger satisfaction
* Analyze differences between satisfied vs dissatisfied passengers
* Provide data-driven recommendations for improving service quality

---

## 📊 Dataset Description

The dataset includes information such as:

* Passenger demographics (age, gender, customer type)
* Flight details (class, distance, travel type)
* Service ratings (WiFi, food, seat comfort, entertainment, etc.)
* Satisfaction label (Satisfied / Neutral / Dissatisfied)

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* **Power BI** → Data cleaning, visualization, and dashboard creation

---

## 📈 Key Insights

Some of the main findings from the analysis:

* ✨ **In-flight WiFi service** and **online boarding experience** are among the strongest drivers of dissatisfaction
* 🧳  Majority of passengers chose our airline for **Business travelers**.
* ⏱️ **Departure and arrival delays** negatively impact satisfaction

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard includes:

* Customer satisfaction overview
* Satisfaction breakdown by class and customer type
* Service quality analysis (WiFi, food, comfort, etc.)
* Delay impact analysis
* Interactive filters for deeper exploration

---

## 🧠 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

1. **Improve WiFi quality and stability**

   * High impact on customer experience
2. **Enhance boarding process efficiency**

   * High-value customers with high expectations
3. **Minimize delays**

   * Strong negative effect on satisfaction
4. **Invest in digital facilities upgrades**

   * Direct impact on long-haul flights
